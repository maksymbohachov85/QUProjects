About us

Our goal is to be helpful

We believe that things exist to make life simpler, more pleasant and kinder. Therefore, the search for the same thing should be fast, convenient and pleasant. We don't just sell clothes, electronics, jewelry, etc. We help you find exactly what you need in one place and without unnecessary worries, so that you don't waste your life searching, but just live happily. Shopaholic is a universal answer to any request, the beginning of the search and its final stop, a real assistant. We forever save our customers from unpleasant compromises, fulfill their wishes and allow them to dream more boldly. With smart search and honest service, we're making our customers' lives a little better right now.

Happiness starts with simple things

And we help to find these things: we remind lovers what to surprise each other with; we motivate athletes to never give up and progress faster; we give the household an opportunity to create real coziness. We want you to know what you're looking for and be able to justify your choice. For this, we shoot video reviews, write articles and track new products.

Convenient delivery

And of course, any product can be ordered with delivery. We deliver in Kyiv within one day, and in Ukraine - the next day. Everything - without prepayment, if necessary - on credit. Payment is cash or non-cash - whichever is more convenient for you.

More later

We want our customers to never have the question "where to find something they need". Therefore, we are now not only the largest online retailer, but also a platform for sellers. Someone will start their first business with us (or expand an existing one), and someone will bring products to Ukraine that are not available yet. This is beneficial for everyone: both buyers and sellers, and even us - because in this way we will help more people become a little happier.
